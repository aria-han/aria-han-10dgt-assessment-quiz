About Digital Art


"Digital art, or the digital arts, is artistic work that uses digital technology as part of the creative or presentational process." Including but not limited to design, animation, and photography, it is a world of endless creative possibilities. Digital art is a subject taught at Rangitoto College, later branching off into various subjects such as design, photography and printmaking. Throughout the course, students learn to work with digital creation apps (e.g. Adobe Photoshop) as well as the general creative process.
